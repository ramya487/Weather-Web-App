DRIZZLE
Drizzle is a type of light precipitation that is characterized by very small, fine droplets of water falling from the skyDrizzle is often associated with cool but damp weather conditions

THINGS TO DO
Take a walk Watch a movie or read a book Cook or bake Work on indoor projects

PRECAUTIONS
Drizzle can make roads and sidewalks slick, so it is important to reduce your speed when driving. It is important to be aware of your surroundings

FOOD HABITS
Consider choosing warm and comforting foods, sipping on hot beverages, and adding heat to your meals with spices and seasonings.

CLOTHING
Warm and dry Waterproof jacket or coat Avoid cotton clothing
