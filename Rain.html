RAIN
Natural phenomenon that occurs when water droplets fall from the atmosphere and reach the ground

THINGS TO DO
Stay indoors. If it is raining heavily outside, it is best to stay indoors to avoid getting wet Enjoy indoor activities.Use the rainy day as an opportunity to enjoy indoor activities such as reading, playing board games, or watching movies

PRECAUTIONS
Stay informed Avoid flooded area Use appropriate transportation Stay away from power lines

FOOD HABITS
Drink warm fluids Consume foods rich in Vitamin C Eat hot and spicy foods Stay hydrated

CLOTHING
Waterproof Water-resistant material, such as a raincoat, poncho, or jacket Dry and comfortable
