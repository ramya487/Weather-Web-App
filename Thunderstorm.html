THUNDERSTORM
A THUNDERSTORM is characterized by thunder and lightning. Thunderstorms can produce heavy rain, strong winds, hail, and tornadoes.

THINGS TO DO
Seek shelter Stay indoors

PRECAUTIONS
During a thunderstorm, stay indoors and avoid using corded phones, electrical equipment, and plumbing.

FOOD HABITS
Avoid opening the refrigerator or freezer unnecessarily, as this can cause food to spoil more quickly.If you plan to cook during a thunderstorm, avoid using electrical appliances such as a stove or oven, as a power surge could damage the appliances or cause a fire. Instead, consider using a gas stove or grill, if it is safe to do so.

CLOTHING
Choose a jacket or raincoat made from waterproof or water-resistant materials such as Gore-Tex or nylon.
